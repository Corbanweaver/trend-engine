# TrendBoard Paid Ads Launch Plan

Use this for the first $100 ad test. The goal is not to scale yet. The goal is to learn which promise gets real creators to sign up, run an analysis, and consider paying.

## Budget

- Google Search: $75
- TikTok traffic test: $25

## Tracking

All links below include UTM parameters. The app stores first-touch and last-touch attribution and sends it with conversion events such as signup, analyze, checkout started, and checkout completed.

Do not remove the UTM parameters from the final URLs.

## What To Measure Today

Good early signals:

- Cost per signup under $8
- At least 20% of signups click Analyze
- At least one checkout started
- Search terms are creator/business intent, not students or job seekers

Bad signals:

- Clicks with no signups
- Search terms are mostly templates, jobs, school, or definitions
- People sign up but do not run an analysis

## After The First $100

Do not increase spend until we review:

- Which UTM source/content drove signups
- Which source drove analyze clicks
- Which source drove checkout starts
- Google search terms report
- Top landing pages by signup rate

Next technical upgrade:

- Add Google Tag / Google Ads conversion tracking after the ad account gives us the conversion ID and label.
- Add TikTok Pixel after the TikTok ad account gives us the pixel ID.

# Affiliate Launch Plan

Last updated: May 23, 2026

## Launch Rule

Start with a small manual program before adding a public affiliate portal. Use creator codes, verify paid customers in admin, and pay only for clean first paid subscriptions.

## Starter Offer

- Payout: $10 per first paid customer, or 25% of the first month if negotiated.
- Customer discount: 20% off the first month is auto-applied when checkout starts from an approved creator link.
- Eligible event: first paid subscription attributed to the creator code.
- Not eligible: free signups, self-referrals, refunds, chargebacks, duplicate accounts, spam, coupon abuse, or paid search on TrendBoard/contentideamaker branded terms.
- Review window: wait 30 days after payment before payout.
- Payment cadence: monthly, after the creator sends payout details privately.

## Required Creator Disclosure

Creators must make the relationship obvious near the link or code. Safe examples:

- "I may earn a commission if you subscribe through my link."
- "Sponsored/referral link for TrendBoard."
- "#ad" or "#sponsored" at the start of a social caption.

Do not bury disclosure at the end of a long caption, hide it only on a separate page, or mix it into a large block of unrelated hashtags.

## Manual Launch Checklist

1. Send the creator their link and this rule: disclose the referral relationship clearly.
2. Ask them not to promise guaranteed growth, revenue, views, or results.
3. Ask them not to bid on TrendBoard or ContentIdeaMaker branded paid search terms.
4. Confirm their first test click lands on the site with `?ref=their-code`.
5. Confirm a real signup with their code appears in `/admin` under Affiliate referrals.
6. Pay only after the first paid subscription survives the review window.
7. Keep payout records outside the repo; do not store tax forms, payment details, or private addresses here.
